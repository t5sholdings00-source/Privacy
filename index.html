Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

T5S Holdings operates as a private correspondence organization.
We collect only what is necessary. We protect what is entrusted.

The Framework
01

Our Privacy Philosophy

T5S Holdings (Top 5ive · Solutions) is a Houston-based organization dedicated to real estate acquisition, social infrastructure, and strategic capital ventures. Our digital presence at wearet5s.com is an informational gateway designed for private correspondence and the evaluation of strategic partnerships.

We do not engage in public-facing advertising or e-commerce. We do not collect payment card information, billing addresses, or consumer shopping histories. Access is earned, not given — and the same principle governs your data.

02

Information We Collect

We limit our data collection to what is adequate, relevant, and reasonably necessary for our disclosed operations.

Voluntary Inquiries: When you contact us via our Private Inquiries email or external connection portals, we collect the personal information you voluntarily provide — such as your name, business affiliation, and contact details.

Administrative Log Data: Our hosting provider automatically collects basic metadata, such as IP addresses and access timestamps, to ensure site security and functionality.

03

Use of Your Personal Information

We process your information exclusively to facilitate the earned access and private correspondence models of our organization. This includes evaluating potential partnerships within our three pillars:

Vindex · Real Estate  ·  Operations · Social Services  ·  Alliance · Strategic Partnerships

We do not use your personal information for targeted advertising or automated profiling.

Data & Rights
04

No Sale of Personal Data

T5S Holdings does not sell your personal data. We define "sale" as the sharing or transferring of personal data to a third party for monetary or other valuable consideration. We only disclose information to:

Vetted Processors: Service providers who assist in our technical operations (e.g., email hosting) and are contractually bound to maintain your data's confidentiality.

Legal Mandates: Authorities where disclosure is required to comply with a legal proceeding or protect the legal rights of T5S Holdings.

05

Sensitive Data & Children

We do not process sensitive personal data — such as health, ethnic, or religious information — or biometric data without your prior, affirmative consent. Our website is not intended for use by children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

06

Your Texas Privacy Rights

Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), Texas residents have the following rights regarding their personal data:

  • Right of Access: To confirm if we are processing your data and to obtain a copy in a portable format.
  • Right of Correction: To rectify inaccuracies in the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of Deletion: To request that we delete data you have provided or that we have obtained about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out: To opt-out of the processing of your data for sales or profiling, should such activities ever occur.
07

Contact & Requests

To exercise these rights, please contact us at our protected inquiry address. We will respond to authenticated requests within 45 days. If a request is denied, you have the right to appeal via the same address; if unsuccessful, you may submit a complaint to the Texas Attorney General.

Private Inquiries info@wearet5s.com
08

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data. However, as our site is used for private correspondence, the transmission of personal information is done at your own risk.
